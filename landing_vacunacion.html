Preparations Before Vaccine


The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's immune system. Threfore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:


Avoid alcoholic beverages
  • Don't drink any alcoholic beverages
Avoid stress

  • Stress has a profound effect on immune work.
    Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol
    production and oxidative stress in the body.

Eat healthy food
  • Don't eat high sugared or junk food. Stay away from soft drinks
Get enough sleep
  • Sleep well before the vaccination
Excercise or physical activity
  • Do it with moderation
